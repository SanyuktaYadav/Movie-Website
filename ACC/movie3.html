Thappad
Thappad (transl/Slap) is a 2020 Indian Hindi-language drama film directed by Anubhav Sinha, which he also co-produced with Bhushan Kumar of T-Series. The film, starring Taapsee Pannu, was released in theatres on 28 February 2020. The film bears a striking similarity to the popular Australian drama series, The Slap, which itself is based off of the The Slap, a 2008 novel by Christos Tsiolkas.
